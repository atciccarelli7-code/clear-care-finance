# Article-style Hospital Discharge Guide Review Notes

This branch intentionally rebuilds the Hospital Discharge & Medicare Quick Guide as a plain-English, article-style printable guide.

## Direction

The guide should read like the clearest Community Acquired Finance articles:

- one concrete question per page,
- short direct answer,
- plain-English context,
- practical questions,
- documents to keep,
- one short call script,
- source note.

## What this is not

- Not a visual-framework guide.
- Not a brochure.
- Not an insurer ranking or plan recommendation page.
- Not a lead form.
- Not a QR/destination-marker release.

## Review standard

A tired family member should be able to understand the page in about a minute while standing outside a hospital room.

Before merge, review the generated PDF on desktop, phone, and black-and-white print.
