# Article-style Quick Guide Review Notes

This branch intentionally rebuilds the Hospital Discharge & Medicare Quick Guide as a plain-English, article-style printable guide and applies the same manuscript standard to the Medicare Sign-Up and Medicaid Application preflight drafts.

## Direction

Every guide should read like the clearest Community Acquired Finance articles:

- one concrete question per page,
- short direct answer,
- plain-English context,
- why the issue matters,
- practical questions to ask,
- documents to keep or gather,
- one short call script or next step,
- source note.

## What this is not

- Not a visual-framework guide.
- Not a brochure.
- Not an insurer ranking or plan recommendation page.
- Not a lead form.
- Not a QR/destination-marker release.

## Guide status

- Hospital Discharge & Medicare Quick Guide: public launch-review candidate in this PR.
- Turning 65 Medicare Sign-Up Quick Guide: article-style preflight manuscript only; do not publish publicly yet.
- Medicaid Application Quick Guide: article-style preflight manuscript only; do not publish publicly yet.

## Review standard

A tired family member should be able to understand the page in about a minute while standing outside a hospital room.

Before public release of any guide PDF, review the generated PDF on desktop, phone, and black-and-white print. Confirm the direct public URL only after release is intentionally approved.
