## Purpose

This matrix evaluates whether Community Acquired Finance helps a visitor complete a real task—not merely whether a route or article exists. Status reflects the current repository plus this workstream. It does not claim that any site output is an official legal, tax, investment, medical, insurance, billing, employment, eligibility, or coverage determination.

### Status definitions

- **Complete:** coherent direct answer, interactive support when appropriate, qualified result, next steps, verification, and strong internal continuity.
- **Strong but improvable:** useful end-to-end experience with identifiable enhancements that do not block the main job.
- **Partial:** meaningful content or tooling exists, but the visitor can still become stranded or must assemble the decision independently.
- **Thin:** route exists but mainly lists content or provides a shallow checklist.
- **Missing:** no adequate canonical experience.
- **Research-gated:** implementation should wait for current authoritative policy research or professional review.

## High-level findings

### Strongest complete product systems

1. Financial Navigator, My Plan, and Financial Foundation Checkup.
2. Benefits Command Center and healthcare-worker total-compensation comparison.
3. Medical Bill Review Toolkit and prior-authorization flow.
4. Hospital-discharge workspace and Turning 65 timeline.

### Highest-value remaining product gaps

1. State-aware Medicaid and long-term-care routing.
2. Childcare / Dependent Care FSA / tax-credit interaction workflow.
3. Standalone Roth-versus-traditional decision support.
4. Observation-versus-inpatient guided flow.
5. Medicare plan verification checklist that unifies existing comparison assets without ranking plans.
6. Safe My Plan actions across more focused tools.
7. Full graphical browser end-to-end regression suite.

### Safety findings

- The site already has substantial Terms, Privacy, Editorial, Disclosures, accessibility, consent, ad-free route, security-header, and publication-readiness infrastructure.
- This workstream adds defense-in-depth URL minimization at the global Google telemetry boundary and makes that behavior build blocking.
- Shared disclaimer language now explicitly includes investment, tax, billing-liability, and official determination boundaries.
- No technical or policy control can guarantee that the owner will never face legal risk. Material legal documents and jurisdiction-specific obligations should receive qualified attorney review.
