# Diagnosis, Explained — Seven-Guide Source-to-Claim Matrix

**Created:** July 22, 2026  
**Products:** AKI, AFib, GI Bleeding, Bowel Obstruction, High Blood Pressure, High Cholesterol, and Kidney Failure  
**Release status:** Clinical-review previews; noindex, nofollow, ad-free, and excluded from the sitemap.

## Source hierarchy

| Tier | Appropriate use | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1 — professional or regulatory authority** | Definitions, diagnostic standards, treatment frameworks, medication eligibility, procedure timing, dialysis or surgery principles, and high-risk escalation claims. | KDIGO, AHA/ACC, ACG, WSES, ACR, KDOQI, FDA, CMS. |
| **Tier 2 — government or specialty patient education** | Plain-language explanation, practical self-management, common questions, and reinforcement of Tier 1 claims. | NIDDK, CDC, MedlinePlus, National Kidney Foundation, American Heart Association patient resources. |
| **Tier 3 — external coverage and usability benchmark** | Completeness, conventional terminology, navigation, readability, and appointment preparation. Never the sole authority for consequential treatment claims. | Mayo Clinic, Cleveland Clinic, WebMD. |

## Acute kidney injury

| Claim domain | Primary authority | Patient-language support | Boundary | Refresh trigger |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Definition, creatinine, urine output, and staging | Current final KDIGO AKI guideline; NICE AKI guideline | NKF AKI education | Explains a clinician-given diagnosis; no interpretation of one creatinine or eGFR. | Final KDIGO 2026 AKI/AKD publication or NICE revision. |
| 2026 evidence state | KDIGO AKI/AKD public-review hub | None | The 2026 document is labeled a **draft**; the 2012 KDIGO guideline remains the current final KDIGO AKI guideline until publication. | Draft becomes final, is withdrawn, or is materially revised. |
| Fluid and perfusion treatment | KDIGO/NICE professional guidance | NKF | No universal drink-more instruction or fluid target. Heart, kidney, liver, pressure, and volume status control. | New fluid-resuscitation or AKI guidance. |
| Medication hold, adjustment, and restart | KDIGO/NICE; drug-specific regulatory material when needed | NKF | No universal sick-day stop list; every change requires an indication and restart owner. | Final KDIGO update, perioperative guidance, or drug safety change. |
| Dialysis indications | KDIGO professional guidance | NKF/NIDDK | No creatinine threshold; life-threatening fluid, electrolyte, acid, toxin, or uremic complications and the whole clinical picture control. | Final KDIGO update. |
| Post-AKI follow-up | KDIGO CKD 2024 and AKI guidance | NKF | Follow-up and CKD risk are described without predicting individual recovery. | Kidney follow-up guideline revision. |

## Cross-product release rule

Passing automated tests establishes structural, technical, and governance readiness. It does not establish clinical approval. Each guide remains a clinical-review preview until a named qualified reviewer records:

1. credentials and conflicts;
2. exact review date and scope;
3. claim-level findings for medicines, procedures, monitoring, and escalation;
4. corrections and their disposition; and
5. the next review date.
