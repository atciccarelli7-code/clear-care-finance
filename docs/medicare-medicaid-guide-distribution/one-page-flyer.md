# Confused about Medicare, Medicaid, rehab, or nursing home costs?

## Start with a plain-English guide built for patients, caregivers, and families.

Hospital discharge and rehab decisions can move fast. Families are often trying to understand Medicare, Medicaid, skilled nursing facility care, home health, long-term care, prior authorization, and bills at the same time.

Community Acquired Finance is building a free guide to help families ask better questions before discharge, during rehab planning, and when medical bills arrive.

## The guide helps explain:

- Medicare vs Medicaid
- Original Medicare vs Medicare Advantage
- Rehab after a hospital stay
- Skilled nursing facility care
- Home health and medical equipment
- Long-term care and custodial care
- Why Medicare can pay and you can still owe money
- What to ask before discharge
- When to contact SHIP, Medicaid, the plan, the facility, or the billing office
