## Post 2 — Checklist angle

Before agreeing to a discharge plan, families should ask:

- Is the patient inpatient, outpatient, or observation?
- What skilled care is being ordered?
- Is rehab approved or still pending authorization?
- Is the facility in-network or Medicare-certified?
- What could the patient owe?
- What happens if coverage ends?

I’m turning these questions into a free 10-page quick guide for families.
