# Community Acquired Finance Monetization Roadmap — July 2026

## Baseline

Analytics export period: Jun 2 6pm through Jul 2 6:59pm.

- Visitors: 181
- Page views: 459
- Views per visitor: 2.54
- Homepage views: 240
- Non-home page views: 219

## Decision

Do not optimize for immediate ad revenue yet. At this traffic level, display ads are unlikely to produce meaningful income and may hurt trust if placed aggressively.

The best near-term money path is:

1. Fix email capture.
2. Publish source-backed search content around high-intent healthcare finance problems.
3. Make calculators and checklists the product surface.
4. Use AdSense lightly only after approval and traffic growth.
5. Evaluate partners only after trust, repeat readership, and clear guardrails exist.

## Revenue ladder

### Phase 0 — Capture

Goal: make every interested reader capturable.

Work:

- Finish Resend sender/domain setup.
- Confirm newsletter signup works for non-owner emails.
- Keep the signup language educational and non-spammy.
- Do not use popups yet.

Why it matters: a small audience is more valuable if some readers can come back.

### Phase 1 — Trust and search

Goal: grow toward 1,000 monthly page views.

Work:

- Publish around pages that already show early interest: tools, healthcare workers, Medicare/Medicaid, articles, Medicare Advantage, patient costs, and medical bill review.
- Add source-backed articles that answer concrete search questions.
- Crosslink articles to relevant calculators and checklists.

Why it matters: search traffic compounds better than one-time sharing.

### Phase 2 — Tool-led growth

Goal: 1,000 to 5,000 monthly page views.

Work:

- Make calculator CTAs more visible.
- Connect every calculator to at least one article and every article to at least one practical next step.
- Add printable/downloadable checklists only after the core page is useful.

Why it matters: tools create repeat use, email intent, and higher-value pages than generic articles.

### Phase 3 — AdSense

Goal: cover site costs without making the site feel like an ad farm.

Rules:

- Nonintrusive placements only.
- No popups.
- No clutter near sensitive patient/insurance content.
- No deceptive button-ad placement.
- Keep trust and readability first.

### Phase 4 — Partnerships

Goal: add higher-value revenue only after credibility is established.

Potential future categories:

- Healthcare career platforms.
- Education resources.
- Low-cost finance tools.
- Benefits education resources.

Guardrails:

- No best-plan claims.
- No aggressive lead generation.
- No product recommendation framing without clear disclosures and educational context.
- No conflict with the site's patient/healthcare-worker trust position.
