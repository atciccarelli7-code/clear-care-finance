## Editorial use rules

- A source link supports preparation and verification; it does not turn CAF into the controlling authority.
- Medicare dollar figures must carry an effective year and their exact unit.
- Medicaid eligibility and benefits remain state specific.
- Good Faith Estimate and No Surprises protections have eligibility and setting limits; do not promise that a pathway applies.
- A provider estimate, network response, or authorization is not a guarantee of final coverage or price.
- If an official source conflicts with CAF copy, remove or correct the CAF claim before expanding the content.
