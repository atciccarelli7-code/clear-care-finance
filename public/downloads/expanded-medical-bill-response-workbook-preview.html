What stays free

Document identification, essential rights, official-source routing, the Medical Bill Response System, and the free Response Pack.

What the workbook adds

Reusable organization, comparison sheets, scripts, deadline control, scenario pathways, and caregiver coordination. It does not determine what someone owes.

Sample page 2 · Start here

How to use this workbook

Use only the pages that match the situation. Keep original notices and documents; this workbook is an organizer, not a replacement.

Privacy: Do not write full account numbers, insurance IDs, Social Security numbers, diagnoses, or detailed medical histories.
Give each visit or billing problem a short neutral reference.
Write every deadline exactly as printed on the notice.
Keep copies of letters, portal messages, forms, and confirmations.
Always free
Rights, official sources, document identification, and basic checklists.
Workbook value
Detailed organization, reusable worksheets, scripts, and deadline control.
Not included
Representation, coding review, legal conclusions, negotiation, or guaranteed savings.
