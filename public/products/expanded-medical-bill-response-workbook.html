Audience validation

Help decide what deserves deeper investment

Early-access interest helps determine whether the expanded workbook solves a real organization problem. No payment is accepted, and Community Acquired Finance will not interpret a bill through this form.

Do not submit bills, diagnoses, provider names, insurance information, account numbers, or other protected information.

What this workbook does not do

  • — Determine the legally correct balance
  • — Review medical coding or clinical records
  • — Negotiate with a provider or insurer
  • — Provide individualized medical, legal, tax, insurance, or financial advice
  • — Guarantee savings, coverage, or appeal outcomes

When payment may be reconsidered

Only after the site has consistent non-founder traffic, verified email delivery and unsubscribe behavior, repeated qualified interest, clear support and refund procedures, and founder approval for a secure hosted provider.

No card information will ever be collected by this page.

Official sources remain free

Rules and processes change. The workbook routes readers to current CMS, HealthCare.gov, IRS, Medicare, CFPB, and state resources instead of presenting itself as the final authority.
